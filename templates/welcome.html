How To Play

Rock, paper, scissors(also known as roshambo) is a hand game usually played between two people.

The shapes used are a rock(a closed fist), paper(a flat hand) and scissors(a fist with index and middle finger extended).

Rock crushes Scissors, Paper covers Rock and Scissors cuts Paper
